Exoplanet Architectures

Yale University

Project: Exploring Planet Nine Analogues with Gaia DR4 and Roman Space Telescope

This research focuses on using data from the Gaia space observatory and the upcoming Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope to search for and characterize distant objects in our solar system, including potential analogues to the hypothesized Planet Nine. The work involves analyzing astrometric data to detect the subtle gravitational influences these distant bodies exert on other objects. My focus is on developing and applying novel statistical techniques to differentiate true signals from observational noise in large datasets.

Key Skills & Techniques

  • Astrometric data analysis
  • Gravitational microlensing & transit photometry
  • Statistical modeling and signal processing
  • High-performance computing
